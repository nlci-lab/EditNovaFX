EditNovaFX
Workflow
  • Upload scripture text, audio, timing, and optional visuals.
  • Create subtitles with AI or import existing SRT files.
  • Place clips and subtitle layers on timeline tracks.
  • Tune subtitle font, size, color, position, and timing.
  • Watch full playback and make quick refinements.
  • Export video locally or publish directly to YouTube.
  • Reuse your setup to scale chapter and book production.
